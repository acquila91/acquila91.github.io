Sound Examples

This webpage provides supplementary materials for the article titled "An Adaptive Wavelet-Based Algorithm for Assessing the Quality of the Attack Transients in Non-Percussive Instruments Sounds", submitted to the International Conference on Digital Audio Effects (DAFx) 2025. These audio samples are provided to enhance understanding of the methods and results discussed in the paper.

Accuracy and Robustness Analysis

The following histograms present the distributions of attack durations estimated using three different methods:

  • Weakest-effort method: A simple approach that estimates attack duration with minimal constraints.
  • -3 dB below the maximum value: Defines the attack duration threshold at -3 dB relative to the peak amplitude.
  • Pitch-synchronous wavelet transform (PSWT) method: A more refined technique that synchronizes analysis with the pitch for improved accuracy.
These methods were applied to a trumpet-like synthesized sound across six different levels of signal-to-noise ratio (SNR): 100, 20, 12, 7, 3, and 1. Each histogram displays the distribution of attack durations, along with the average value and standard deviation for each method.
