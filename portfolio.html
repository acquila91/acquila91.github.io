Welcome to my portfolio. Here, I present selected projects that showcase my dedication to enhancing learning experiences through innovative technology and design.

MEI-Trumpet

An educational web application designed to assist music students in mastering trumpet fingering and sound production techniques.

Features:

  • Customizable exercises with adjustable tempo and note range.
  • Step-by-step guidance with visual fingering charts.
  • Integrated chromatic tuner and dynamic markings.

My Contributions: Led the development and design, conducted user research, and implemented core functionalities.

Hearing-Care Platform

A mobile application dedicated to personalized noise exposure assessment and hearing health promotion.

Features:

  • Real-time noise level monitoring during daily activities.
  • Visualization tools for overall sound exposure analysis.
  • Estimation of the 'Age of Your Ears' based on exposure data.

My Contributions: Collaborated on UI/UX design, conducted usability studies, and refined features based on user feedback.
